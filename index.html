The final variation introduces the idea of percentiles to the visualization. If a country is on the red line, then it is in the 25th percentile (as indicated by the legend) for the shown data column. This means that it is part of the lowest 25% of all the countries for the shown data column. The location of the left side of the flag of a country determines the percentile group it belongs to.
